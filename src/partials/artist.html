    About me

    I’m Ruslan, a tattoo artist since 2020. I started in Ukraine and now work in Canada, with 4 years of experience. My focus is realism dotwork, using just black pigment and a specific needle configuration to create high-quality, textured tattoos that last. I’m passionate about portraits and realism, constantly honing my skills. I also enjoy graphic work, as that’s how I began. Tattooing brings me great joy, and my love for it drives me to keep evolving.
